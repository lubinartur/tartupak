# Product Catalog Structure

Product categories for the Tartupak website.

## Transport Packaging

Standard corrugated cardboard transport boxes used for shipping and storage.

Applications:
- logistics
- warehouses
- shipping

---

## Pizza Boxes

Food-safe cardboard pizza boxes for restaurants and delivery services.

Applications:
- restaurants
- pizza delivery
- takeaway food

---

## Frozen Products Packaging

Moisture-resistant packaging designed for frozen products.

Applications:
- frozen food production
- cold storage logistics

---

## Custom Packaging

Packaging developed specifically for the customer's product dimensions
and transportation conditions.

Applications:
- industrial products
- electronics
- fragile items

---

## Large Packaging

Heavy-duty corrugated cardboard packaging for large or heavy goods.

Applications:
- industrial equipment
- pallet transport

---

## Printed Packaging

Flexo printed cardboard packaging with logos or branding.

Applications:
- retail packaging
- brand packaging
