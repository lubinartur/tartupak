# Content Guidelines

## Tone of voice

Professional
Clear
Reliable
Not too corporate
Not too emotional

---

## Main message

Tartupak produces cardboard and corrugated packaging for business clients.

The website should explain:

• what Tartupak produces
• who it is for
• how to request custom packaging
• why clients should trust Tartupak

---

## Copy principles

Use short paragraphs.

Avoid generic claims like:
"best quality" or "innovative solutions" without context.

Prefer concrete language:

Good:
"Send us the dimensions, quantity and intended use — we will suggest a suitable packaging solution."

Bad:
"We provide innovative and high-quality packaging solutions for every need."
